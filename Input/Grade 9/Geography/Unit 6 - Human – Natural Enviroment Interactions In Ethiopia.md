The factors influencing soil erosion and, therefore, land degradation are multiple and mutually reinforcing. As indicated earlier, the massive removal of vegetative cover is the driving force behind land degradation. This loss is largely due to an expanding population, with its corresponding increased demand for crops, grazing land, and fuel wood.
